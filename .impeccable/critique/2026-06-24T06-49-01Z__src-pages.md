## Priority Issues

**P0 (blocking)**
- None found. No flow is unusable; nothing blocks a sighted, mouse-using collector from completing core jobs.

**P1 (major)**
- Inconsistent confirmation pattern for destructive/high-impact actions: record delete (RecordDetail.jsx ~500, backdrop-click dismiss + no Escape handler), role change (UserTable.jsx ~104-114, plain `<select onChange>` with no confirm step), vs. collection wipe (Admin.jsx, type-to-confirm). - Keyboard/focus gaps on primary search & browse flows: Home.jsx's search-suggestion dropdown is mouse-only (`onBlur` + 150ms `setTimeout`, `SuggestionItem` has no `tabIndex`); AllRecords.jsx search input has no associated `<label>`.
